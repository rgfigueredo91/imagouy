Imago was founded in 2018 by Andres Baptista and Rodrigo Figueredo. We are a Rendering and 3dart imaging studio based in Montevideo, Uruguay. We produce Trusted architectural visualization services for busineses and architecture professionals. We are specialized in developing Architectural Renderings, 3D Architecture, Computer Graphics Images and any kind of Architectural Visualization in the field of architecture, urban planning and civil engineering.
